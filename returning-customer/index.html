Returning Customers

Thank You for choosing us to continue to serve you and your boat's needs.

After more than 30 years of service Van Slooten Harbour Marina has changed hands to become Bridgeview Harbour Marina. We have many exciting plans for the coming years and hope you are as excited as we are to kick off the 2021 Boating Season.

This year we are introducing online reservations & billing through Dockwa. Dockwa provides marina customers with an easy to use, app (links below) and web based method of requesting services, reserving dockage/slips (at our marina and others), reviewing, signing, and referencing contracts, updating user or boat information, and paying bills online or in person.

If you are a social media user, Check out our new Facebook & Instagram Pages.
You should also request to join our Private Marina Members Facebook Group which is a great way to chat with other marina members, coordinate events, and see updates about whats happening at the marina.

Please use the buttons below to choose they type of dockage you would like this season.

If you don't see the option you are looking for, or you would like more information please reach out to us at crew@bridgeviewharbour.com
