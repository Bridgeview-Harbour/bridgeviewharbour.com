Returning Customers

Thank You for choosing us to continue to serve you and your boat's needs.

After more than 30 years of service Van Slooten Harbour Marina has changed hands to become Bridgeview Harbour Marina. We have many exciting plans for the coming years and hope you are as excited as we are to kick off the 2021 Boating Season.

We have introduced Online Reservations & Billing through Dockwa an app (iOS and Android) and Web based marina mangement tool.
Dockwa provides marina customers with an easy to use, online method of requesting services, reserving dockage/slips (at our marina and others), reviewing, signing, and referencing contracts, updating user or boat information, and paying bills online or in person.
