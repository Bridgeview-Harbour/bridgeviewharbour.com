Winter Storage and Service

With Labor Day behind us and the 2021 boating season coming to a close, it is time to begin thinking about the preparations for the autumn and winter seasons.

Bridgeview Harbour Marina provides Indoor and Outdoor Storage, Shrinkwrapping, and Boat Winterization services to ensure your vessel (or other item) has a restful winter hibernation.
2021 - 2022 Storage and Shrink-Wrap Pricing:
